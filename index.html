Community Pharmacy — What I Bring

Community pharmacy is where clinical knowledge meets human connection — and that intersection is where I thrive. Working at a GAHAR-accredited pharmacy has sharpened my ability to deliver care that is both evidence-based and deeply personal. I counsel patients on chronic disease management, navigate complex drug interaction profiles, and translate dense medical information into clear, actionable guidance people can actually follow.

Beyond the counter, I focus on outcomes — not just dispensing, but ensuring medication adherence improves, patients return with fewer complications, and the pharmacy operates as a trusted first point of care. My clinical rotations across oncology, hepatology, and infection control give me a diagnostic lens that most community pharmacists don't have — allowing me to catch what others might miss.
